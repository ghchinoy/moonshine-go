browser-listen — moonshine serve, no install required

← back to samples · Captures your microphone with getUserMedia + AudioWorklet, streams raw PCM to a running moonshine serve over a WebSocket binary frame, and renders the live transcript coming back over the same connection. No build step, no dependency of any kind — open this file, or serve it, in a browser.
